CERBERUS - Anti-Theft IoT Application

Cerberus is an Android Application that works together with a developed IoT hardware to provide anti-theft capabilities for motorcycle. With the use of Telegram API, IprogTech SMS API, BigDataCloud API, and Open Street Map, the Android Application can track the location of the motorcycle and be notified on the actions made on the vehicle.

What does CERBERUS do for preventing motorcycle theft?

CERBERUS acts as the owner's command center, bringing ignition control, live tracking, image evidence, and fingerprint management into a single interface. Built with Firebase Realtime Database for real-time syncing, the app keeps owners informed and in control of their motorcycle's security from anywhere.

Secure App Access

Before reaching any of the motor's controls, users authenticate through a PIN-protected login screen, with fingerprint login available as a quicker alternative. This keeps the app itself locked down, so even if a phone is lost or borrowed, motor control stays out of reach of anyone without the correct credentials.

One-Tap Motor Control

The Home dashboard gives users a real-time snapshot of the motorcycle's current status, including its last known location and the exact time it was last updated. A single Activate button lets the owner switch the motor's electrical circuit ON or OFF remotely, with the current state reflected immediately on screen.

Notification Feed

Every event, motor commands, fingerprint authentications, device heartbeats, and theft alerts, arrives as its own entry in the Notifications tab, complete with timestamps and the exact location where it occurred. Users can search past notifications or filter them by date to trace a specific event after the fact.

Live Location Tracking

The Location tab plots the motorcycle's current position on an interactive map, updating as the device reports new coordinates. This gives owners a direct way to confirm where their motorcycle is at any given moment, without cross-referencing GPS data manually.

Dashcam Image Gallery

Captured images from the onboard camera are organized into a dedicated Dashcam gallery, grouped by date for easy browsing. Users can select multiple images at once to download or delete them, giving them control over how evidence is reviewed and stored.

In-App Security Settings

The Security tab lets users manage their own protections directly from the app, changing their PIN or adding and removing enrolled physical motor fingerprints without needing physical access to the device. This keeps credential and fingerprint management fully self-serviceable.
