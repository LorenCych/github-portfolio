Dental clinic appointment management system

This project is a web-based appointment management system catered for a local dental clinic at Oriental Mindoro. The project is developed using PHP Laravel, partnered with Iprogtech SMS API, and Brevo Email Services. The system follows an MVC (Model-View-Controller) architecture and uses Supabase for the development of its database.

How does the Appointment Management System Solve Things?

The appointment management system caters on both the clinic's patients and dentists. With this system, patient's are given the chance to check what the clinic can offer, verify the availability dentist on a particular schedule, and reduce the problems caused by walk-in appointments. Informative Landing Page

The landing page provides essential details about the clinic, including operating hours, contact information, and service offerings. It also features an integrated contact form for inquiries, an embedded map for easy navigation, and a brief background about the clinic, ensuring that potential patients can easily learn more and reach out.

Platform for Booking Appointments

The system offers an intuitive platform where patients can conveniently book appointments based on available slots and services. This reduces manual scheduling errors and provides patients with immediate feedback on appointment status. Rescheduling appointments are also made easier, being worth just a few clicks.

Actively Notifies Users

Upon appointment creation, patient's immediately receive an SMS. Moreover, Both patients and dentists receive automated notifications via email regarding appointment confirmations, updates, or cancellations. This ensures that everyone stays informed and reduces the chances of missed or double-booked appointments

Intuitive Appointment Management Experience

The system is equipped with a clear and organized dashboard that allows dentists and administrators to view, accept, or manage appointments effortlessly. Real-time updates ensure all parties have access to the latest information, improving coordination within the clinic. Search bars and filters are also present to make things easier.

Role-based Access for Administrators

The application enforces role-based access control to ensure security and proper data handling. Administrators have full control over system settings, appointment approvals, and user management, while dentists and patients have access only to the features relevant to their roles.

Responsive and Accessible on Any Device

The system is fully responsive, allowing users to book and manage appointments seamlessly whether they’re on a desktop, tablet, or smartphone. This ensures convenience for patients and staff, enabling them to access the platform anytime and anywhere.
