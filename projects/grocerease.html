Grocerease: Inventory management system

GrocerEase is a web-based inventory management system developed using ASP.NET Core technology. The system follows the MVC (Model-View-Controller) architecture and uses Entity Framework for the development of its database.

What Can GrocerEase Offer?

Through GrocerEase, inventory management will become faster and smarter, providing users the ability to create more well-informed decisions in stock management, saving them time and money.

Product Categorization

Organize products into smart categories and monitor each category's inventory health and sales performance at a glance.

Convenient Stock Tracking

Instantly know which products need restocking, are overstocked, expired, or nearing expiry. All these without the hassle of manual checking.

Product Transactions

Every stock movement is automatically logged. GrocerEase ensures an accurate, traceable history of product inflows and outflows, minimizing stock inconsistencies.

Supplier Management

Record and manage suppliers and alternate sources per product to ensure uninterrupted supply even during urgent procurement needs.

Printable Reports

Easily generate comprehensive reports with key inventory and sales details to support business insights, analysis, or liquidation.

Intuitive User Interface

GrocerEase is designed with simplicity and clarity in mind. Every feature is laid out with ease of use as a priority, ensuring users can get things done without getting lost.
