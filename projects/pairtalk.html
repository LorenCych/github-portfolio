PairTalk: SIngle Session Messaging APplication

PairTalk is a lightweight chat app designed for two users sharing a remote desktop session. Instead of switching to a separate messaging app or relying on an unstructured notes app, PairTalk keeps communication on-screen, ensuring seamless and distraction-free collaboration without needing another device.

What is PairTalk All About?

PairTalk makes it easy for two users sharing a remote desktop session to communicate by providing a messaging space that is easily accessible, clean, and straightforward.

Real-Time Messaging in Shared Sessions

Communicate seamlessly between two users within the same remote desktop environment. No need to switch devices or open separate apps.

Familliar Messaging Interface

PairTalk adapts an interface comprised of color-coded chat bubbles with user-specific avatars and alignment to clearly distinguish messages between Local and Remote users.

Timestamped Messages for Both Users

Every message is automatically marked with a timestamp to keep track of the flow of conversation during the session.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Efficient keyboard shortcuts for sending messages, switching users, and clearing chat make the app fast and keyboard-friendly.

Copyable Chat & Subtle Sound Feedback

Users can copy text directly from the conversation, with soft sound cues on actions to enhance interactivity without being disruptive.

Customizable Themes

Supports light and dark themes with an intuitive settings menu for a personalized chat experience.
